About Me

You guessed it right! I want to give myself a fresh start as a web developer and this website is my portfolio. I have created it to demonstrate my knowledge and skills

I am an ambitious front-end web developer with a solid foundation that will allow me to progress quickly. My previous experience in business will contribute to project work and client relationships. Diligence, high intelligence, and friendly attitude toward people will make me a reliable member of my new team.

I was a successful executive for many years and dealt with computer technology from a managerial point of view. I have always been distinguished by an impeccable business reputation, independent thinking, the ability to analyze information and implement results into business processes.

My life changed drastically when my family moved to a new country. I took it not only as a challenge but also as an opportunity. Not afraid to start all over again, I got the chance to embody myself where I am strongest.

Why did I decide to become a web developer?

Firstly, I like to see how a problem that initially seems to be a formless lump reveals logical relationships, then becomes a system and transforms into code, and finally is born on the screen. I always get aesthetic pleasure when I come across a good website.

Secondly, having extensive experience in business, I clearly understand my strengths and weaknesses. My intuition tells me that the web developer profession is where I will be valuable to the company and thriving as a professional for many years.

And now, I am proud to share my first achievement in a new profession - graduating from Wilmington University and receiving a bachelor's degree in computer science with Summa Cum Laude title. In addition, I am gaining additional practice by studying the "Front-End Engineer" course at Codecademy.

It means that an exciting journey is waiting for me, and my motivation and hard work give me confidence that I will achieve my goals.
