About Me

I am an ambitious front-end web developer with a solid foundation in website development and system integration. My previous experience in business management significantly enhances my project work and client relationships. Diligence, intelligence, and a friendly attitude make me a reliable team member. With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Wilmington University, where I graduated summa cum laude , I have extensive hands-on experience in front-end development, particularly in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React and Bootstrap. My proficiency extends to robust API integrations, troubleshooting, and providing technical guidance.

My journey into web development started after a successful career in business. As the Director of ZMH Ltd in Ukraine, I led the company to rank 6th in the market and implemented strategies that reduced order delivery time by 76%. This role honed my skills in project management, business process development, and efficiency analysis.

A significant life change occurred when my family moved to a new country. I embraced this challenge as an opportunity to start anew in a field that aligns with my strengths and passions.

Why did I decide to become a web developer? Firstly, I find great satisfaction in transforming complex problems into logical systems and code that result in functional and aesthetically pleasing websites. Secondly, my business background gives me a clear understanding of leveraging my strengths in this field, ensuring I bring value to my company. I am proud to have completed several web development projects, including a dynamic memory game and a classic arcade game clone, both available on my GitHub. Additionally, I am enhancing my skills through the "Front-End Engineer" course at Codecademy.

An exciting journey awaits me in the web development field, and my motivation and hard work give me confidence that I will achieve my goals and contribute effectively to innovative projects.
