ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Igor Mykhalchuk.

I've created this website to demonstrate my knowledge and skills as a web developer. You guessed it right, I want to start a new career and this website is my portfolio.

There have been different stages in my life. For many years I was a successful manager and was engaged in computer technology from the managerial point of view. My life changed drastically when our family has moved to a new country. This change was not only a challenge but also an opportunity. I was not afraid to start all over again and got the chance to embody myself where I am strongest. I have always been distinguished by independent thinking, the ability to analyze information and build logical systems, and an impeccable business reputation. Why do I want to become a web developer? There are two reasons. Firstly, I get aesthetic satisfaction when I see a good website. It gives me pleasure to think, write code and see its visual interpretation. Secondly, having many years of experience in business, I clearly understand my strengths and weaknesses. My intuition tells me that a web developer profession is mine. Here I will be both useful and successful. If it requires a lot of work and study - I'm ready! Now I work as a bus driver and finish my studies at the university with a degree in computer science. In addition, I'm gaining practical knowledge by taking the "Front-End Engineer" course at Codecademy. I am sure that I will achieve the goal, and the golden key to it is my motivation and diligence.
